Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with VedSAAS?
Simply sign up for a free account, choose your preferred AI mode, and start chatting! No credit card required for the free plan.
Which languages are supported?
VedSAAS supports 22+ Indian languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, and more.
How do I upgrade my plan?
Go to your account settings and click on "Upgrade Plan". You can choose between Pro and Enterprise plans based on your needs.
Is my data secure?
Yes! We use end-to-end encryption and never share your data with third parties. Your conversations are completely private.
