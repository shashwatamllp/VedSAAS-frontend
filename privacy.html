Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

At VedSAAS, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI platform and services.

TL;DR: We collect minimal data, encrypt everything, never sell your information, and give you full control over your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Optional profile picture, language preferences
  • Conversation Data: Messages you send to our AI
  • Payment Information: Billing details (processed securely via third-party providers)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Improve our AI models (with your consent)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Strict role-based access controls
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on secure, compliant servers
Privacy-First Architecture: You can run VedSAAS on your own server for 100% data privacy. Your conversations never leave your infrastructure.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your conversation history
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Object: Object to certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@vedsaas.com

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide you services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we permanently delete your data within 30 days, except where required by law.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve our services

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

VedSAAS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

VedSAAS is operated from India. If you access our services from outside India, your data may be transferred to and processed in India. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

We comply with GDPR for European users and applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our platform. Continued use of VedSAAS after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@vedsaas.com
  • Support: support@vedsaas.com
  • Website: vedsaas.com
